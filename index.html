Spiritual Gifts Survey

Discover your spiritual gifts

This survey will help you to understand how God has gifted you.

It is not a test, so there are no wrong answers.

The survey consists of 80 statements; you will rate how accurately each statement describes you. Some statements reflect concrete actions, other statements are descriptive traits, and still others are statements of belief.

  • Select the one response you feel best characterizes yourself.
  • Do not spend too much time on any one item. Remember, it is not a test. Usually your immediate response is best.
  • Please give an answer for each item. Do not skip any items.
  • Do not ask others how they are answering or how they think you should answer.
  • Work at your own pace.
